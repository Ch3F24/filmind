about us

Filmind is the result of a long-term collaboration between Mythberg Films and TheScout. Based in Budapest, Hungary, Filmind offers unique solutions for full service and co-productions, locations & sets along with experienced and skilled professionals.

cash rebate

Since almost 30 years Hungary offers a stable and reliable 30% cash rebate system to film productions, including cinema features, animations, tv films and series, documentaries and even shorts. No minimum spend, no cap, no delivery obligation and broad scale of eligible costs from develoment to post-production.
